About Us

Dayna Spirite, the founder and owner of Feline Friendly Portraits is a Tampa, Florida photographer who specializes in unique stylized photography, commercial projects and fine art. Danielle holds an AA in Photography and a BA in Computer Science, and has almost two decades of photography experience. She brings a unique and professional highly stylized look to anything from corporate photography, to band photos, to family portraits.

Dayna currently resides in FL with her husband and their beloved cats. She previously worked in the Sony Pictures Entertainment Photo Department before moving to an Arts College in Hollywood to take a position running the photo department. Dayna has a professional photo studio in Tampa where she shoots most of her portraits.

Learn more about Dayna's pet portraits on the pet portraits info page, or check out Dayna's corporate photoshoots and licensing.
